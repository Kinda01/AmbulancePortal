Emergency Services Help

If you're experiencing a medical emergency and have called for an ambulance, here are some steps to take:

  • Stay calm and provide accurate information to the dispatcher.
  • Give your exact location and any specific details to help the ambulance find you quickly.
  • Follow the dispatcher’s instructions carefully.
  • Keep the patient's airway clear and perform basic first aid if instructed by the dispatcher.
  • Stay with the patient until the ambulance arrives.
  • Provide the ambulance team with any medical history and current symptoms.

For non-life-threatening cases, please consider alternative transport methods to avoid overwhelming emergency services.
